Typography for Ember

Five directions — each applied to the same Ember palette mockups

The current Geist + Geist Mono is clean but generic. For a mediation tool with a warm palette, the typography should reinforce the feeling of safety and insight. The big question: serif headings or all sans-serif?

The Options

D Source Serif 4 Source Sans 3 Professional trust Academic journal, approachable
E Newsreader Plus Jakarta Sans Modern authority Publication meets product
Key Question

A serif heading font adds literary weight — it says "this is about meaning, not just data." But it can also feel slower, more editorial. An all-sans system says "this is a tool." The Ember palette already provides warmth, so a sans-serif won't feel cold here. The question is: do you want the typography to reinforce the warmth (serif), or provide contrast to it (clean sans)?

Recommendation

IBM Plex Mono has the warmest character of the three — its curves have a subtle humanist quality that matches the Ember palette. JetBrains Mono is more legible at 8-9px but feels more "code editor." DM Mono is the safe match if you go with DM Sans for body.
