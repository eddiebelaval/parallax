Orb + Waveform SystemParticipant Presence

Every voice in the room gets a living, breathing identity

Each participant — Person A, Person B, Claude — has their own orb. The orb's waveform reflects their current state: idle, speaking, listening, thinking (Claude), or emotionally heated. The orb color matches their temperature-coded identity. Adding a third person just means adding another orb.

Design Rules
COLOR = IDENTITY

Person A = hot/coral. Person B = warm/amber. Claude = cool/teal. Always. The orb color matches their message border color.

WAVEFORM = STATE

Idle: gentle drift. Speaking: energetic oscillation. Listening: soft response. Thinking (Claude): complex layered rhythm. Heated: high amplitude, fast.

GLOW = INTENSITY

Glow radius and breathing speed scale with emotional state. Calm = dim slow pulse. Heated = bright fast pulse. The orb radiates the emotion.

State Transitions

States flow naturally: A person's orb goes idle -> speaking -> idle on their turn. Claude's goes idle -> analyzing -> insight -> idle after each message. When emotional temperature rises, the person's orb shifts to heated (faster pulse, wider glow, higher amplitude waveform). All transitions are smooth CSS — no hard cuts.

Waveform DetailAnimation Anatomy

Each state has a distinct waveform rhythm and amplitude

Idle — Gentle Drift

Low amplitude, slow period (3s). Like breathing at rest. Almost a flat line with a slight shimmer.

amplitude: 2px | period: 3s | easing: ease-in-out
Speaking — Energetic Oscillation

High amplitude, fast period (0.8s). Active, alive. Two overlapping paths (primary + secondary ghost) for depth.

amplitude: 12px | period: 0.8s | dual-path with 0.15s offset
Thinking (Claude) — Complex Rhythm

Medium amplitude, medium period (2s). More frequency nodes than speaking — feels like processing, not emoting. Unique to Claude.

amplitude: 6px | period: 2s | 5 nodes (vs 3 for speaking) | 0.4s offset
Heated — High Intensity

Maximum amplitude, fastest period (0.5s). Erratic, urgent. The orb glow is at its brightest and widest here. Only triggers at emotionalTemperature > 0.7.

amplitude: 18px | period: 0.5s | stroke-width: 2 | glow: breathe-heated
